Game Overview

Racing Limits is an adrenaline-pumping street racing game that combines stunning visual graphics with realistic driving physics. Take control of high-performance vehicles, customize them to perfection, and compete in intense races through diverse urban environments.

Key Features

  • Realistic driving physics
  • Extensive car customization
  • Multiple racing modes
  • Dynamic weather system
  • Career progression system

Racing Modes

Career Mode

Progress through challenging races, unlock new cars, and become a street racing legend.

Jump straight into the action with instant races against AI opponents.

Time Trial

Test your skills and set new track records in solo time trials.
